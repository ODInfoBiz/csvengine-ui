About

The CSV Engine is a project by the Institute for Information Business at WU Vienna (Vienna University of Economics and Business). It is a collection of tools and services for processing and enriching CSV files.

This is a research project and we try to add more features and fix bugs. Any feedback, bug reports, and other comments are very welcome. Please use the following email address for contact:
